Upcoming Meetings

Our technical meetings are nearly always held the last Thursday of every month, from 7pm-9pm. Traditionally we are always an in-person only meeting. Due to COVID, we were forced to adopt virtual meetings as an alternative. As for which meetings will be in-person or virtual, usually you will need to check our Mastodon or ask on Discord. Sometimes the decision is made day of.

Since in person meetings depend on city infection levels, our Mastodon page may have more up-to-date information on meeting statuses.

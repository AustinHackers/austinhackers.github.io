## How to reach us

Should you need to make contact with AHA, you can use one of the following and decide which best suits your needs and sensitivity level.

- **Email**: [aha@takeonme.org](mailto:aha@takeonme.org)
- **PGP**: Email us from your [Proton Mail](https://proton.me/support/proton-mail-encryption-explained) to ours
- **Chat**: Ask for assistance in one of our public [chat](chat) options
